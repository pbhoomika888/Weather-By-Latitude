Latitude vs Max Temperature

Temperature is a physical property of matter that quantitatively expresses hot and cold. Temperature is the manifestation of thermal energy, present in all matter, which is the source of the occurrence of heat, a flow of energy, when a body is in contact with another that is colder.


There is a large correlation between latitude and temperature. Cities With latitudes of -60 to 40 have maximum temperatures greater than 40 degrees Celsius.
